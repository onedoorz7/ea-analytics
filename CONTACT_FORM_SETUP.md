# Contact Form Setup Instructions

Your contact page is ready! Just one quick step to activate the form.

## Activate the Form (2 minutes)

1. Go to https://web3forms.com
2. Enter your email: **info@ea-analytics.com**
3. Click "Get Access Key"
4. Check your email and copy the access key
5. Open `contact.html` in your editor
6. Find this line (around line 234):
   ```html
   <input type="hidden" name="access_key" value="YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE">
   ```
7. Replace `YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE` with your actual key
8. Save and push to GitHub

**That's it!** Form submissions will arrive at info@ea-analytics.com.

## What You Get

- **4 Contact Options:**
  - Embedded contact form (sends to your email)
  - Direct email link
  - Google Calendar booking link
  - LinkedIn profile link

- **Mobile Responsive:** Works perfectly on phones
- **Spam Protection:** Web3Forms includes honeypot and reCAPTCHA
- **Free Forever:** Up to 250 submissions/month (upgradable if needed)

## Form Fields

The form captures:
- Name (required)
- Email (required)
- Company (optional)
- Topic/Interest (dropdown)
- Message (required)

All submissions include a subject line: "New Contact Form Submission from EA Analytics"
