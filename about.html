Background

Before analytics, I worked in film production, coached football, and worked with youth. I come from a creative household, but I'm an analytical person. I combine being methodical, while always making room for creativity.

Some of the best insights come from applying creative thinking within a structured framework.

From Startup to IPO

I joined Riskified's New York office in 2018 as a data analyst when it was still a growing startup. I worked closely with customer-facing teams and got to experience American business culture firsthand.

Back in Israel, I became a product analyst - the data person for a "squad", a startup within the company. I defined how data was saved, transformed, and used. I built dashboards, defined KPIs, and ran analyses that shaped product decisions.

Later, I became an IC - tackling strategic initiatives across the organization. That role taught me I could deliver key insights in new domains, fast.

I stayed through the IPO, gaining hands-on experience within the strict expectations of a publicly traded company.

"That's the generalist's advantage — the ability to deliver key insights within new domains and relatively short timeframes."
